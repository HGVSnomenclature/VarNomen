Sequence Variant Nomenclature

This site covers HGVS-nomenclature, the recommendations for the description of sequence variants. It is used to report and exchange information of variants found in DNA, RNA and protein sequences and serves as an international standard. When using the recommendations please cite: Den Dunnen et al. 2016, Hum.Mutat. 37:564-569. HGVS-nomenclature is authorised by the Human Genome Variation Society (HGVS), the Human Variome Project (HVP) and the Human Genome Organization (HUGO). Please support our efforts:
